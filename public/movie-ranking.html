The Best Action Series According to Onalo

My top 3 action series of all time.

Prison Break

This is one of the greatest action and thriller series ever made.
I enjoy the intelligent planning, unexpected plot twists, and the determination of the characters to overcome impossible situations.

Reacher

Reacher combines intense action, mystery, and justice in every episode.
The main character is calm, highly intelligent, and always ready to protect those who cannot protect themselves.

The Night Agent

The Night Agent is a fast-paced political action thriller filled with suspense, conspiracies, and exciting missions.
Every episode keeps you guessing and wanting to watch the next one.
